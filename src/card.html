Cheetah

They can run 70 mph (or 110 kph), which is as fast as cars drive on the highway.

  • Scientific Name: Acinonyx jubatus
  • Average Lifespan: 10-12 years
  • Habitat: open plains, woodland
  • Weight: 35-60kg

Cheetahs are big cats, members of the Felidae family and closely related to bobcats, lynxes and pumas. These graceful animals are identified by their unique black spots on gold or yellow coats and are known for their amazing speed. In fact the cheetah is the world's fastest land mammal.
